Dharmam

Your Dharma means a purpose in your life.
Ancient yoga texts describe dharma as an inner wisdom, or a cosmic guidance that governs living beings as individuals.
An individual's duty is fulfilled by observance of custom or law.
Dharma of humans is to follow the law of nature.
Knowledge, Patience, Justice, Love And Dedication are the 5 main pillars of dharma.

Practice of Dharmam:-
* Find the main purpose of your life and practise it regularly
